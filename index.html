Workshop on Ocean Turbulence

22 - 26 January 2018 at UKZN, Durban.

Register Now
Learn more ▼

Rockland Scientific and the Fluid Mechanics Lab in Civil Engineering at UKZN are presenting a 5 day workshop on ocean turbulence with theoretical, practical and data processing units. The workshop is sponsored by the United State's Office of Naval Research.

Workshop fees are ZAR 500. Students can apply for a bursary to cover fee and travel costs.

Register until 10th January 2018

Session Contents

Two to four units are taught each day, the VMP-250 Turbulence Profiler will be introduced to demonstrate the measurement of micro-scale turbulence.

A special session is planned for Friday, which will include scientific presentations on ocean research in South Africa.

(1) Fundamental Turbulence Theory

(2) Ocean Turbulence Theory

(3) Introduction to the VMP-250

(4) Data Acquisition Software (ODAS5IR)

(5) Pre-deployment Checks

(6) Ocean Turbulence Measurement Techniques

(7) Ocean Turbulence Measurement Sensors

(8) VMP-250 Deployment

(9) VMP-250 Maintenance

(10) Data Conversion and Processing

(11) Signal Conditioning

(12) Advanced Techniques in Data Processing
